About

5 Seconds of Summer are an Australian pop rock band from Sydney, New South Wales, formed in late 2011 having just released their latest album "5SOS5" in September of 2023.

Meet The Members

  • Luke Hemmings; lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist
  • Calum Hood; bassist
  • Michael Clifford; lead guitarist
  • Ashton Irwin; drummer

5SOS5

5SOS5 is the fifth studio album featuring 19 tracks!

Preceded by the release of the singles 'Complete Mess', 'Take My Hand', 'Me Myself and I', 'Blender', and 'Older'.

5SOS5 debuted at number 2 on the Billboard 200 with 48,000 equivalent ablum units sold in its first week in the US.
